Workflow

How I approach technical and security problems.

This is the operating style I want visible: evidence first, clear prioritization, useful escalation, and reusable documentation.

Understand impact

Identify affected users, blocked work, business impact, urgency, and whether the issue is support, security, network, or system related.

Collect evidence

Review symptoms, logs, screenshots, ticket history, packet captures, tools, affected assets, and repeat behavior.

Classify risk

Separate normal operational noise from recurring failures, suspicious behavior, exposed services, misconfigurations, or actionable findings.

Resolve or escalate

Fix what is in scope, escalate with context when needed, and make sure the next person has enough evidence to act quickly.

Document for reuse

Write notes and summaries that make the next troubleshooting attempt faster, clearer, and easier to validate.
